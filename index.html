FIRST 1,000 USERS
1. Beta via 5 gym WhatsApp communities. 2. Referral loop — invite = streak shield. 3. Fitness micro-influencer seeding (10–50K).
POSITIONING
"The only fitness app that makes your real life feel like a cultivation novel." Duolingo + Shonen anime + LinkedIn for the body.
MONETISATION PRIORITY
Month 1: Free + recipe marketplace. Month 3: Season Pass. Month 6: Brand challenges + rare title prize pools.
KEY RISKS & MITIGATIONS
Cold start → gym community seeding. Recipe fraud → verified creator badge. Prize pool abuse → multi-layer verification system.

1,000 users. D7 retention ≥ 40%. 50+ recipes posted. 1 rare title contested. 2.3 avg active challenges per user.
