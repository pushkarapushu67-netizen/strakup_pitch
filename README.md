# StreakUp — Become Legendary

A 0→1 product concept for a gamified fitness app where your real body becomes your character.

---

### What is StreakUp?
Most fitness apps track your workouts. StreakUp turns your life into a cultivation novel.
Upload 4 photos, get a Donghua-style 3D avatar built from your actual physique.
Every rep, every streak, every milestone earns you a Title — some only 1 person on Earth can hold.

---

### What's in this repo
- Full interactive product concept (single HTML file)
- AI Avatar Builder mockup
- RPG stats system tied to real fitness metrics
- Titles & Honours system with prize pools
- Recipe marketplace
- Global leaderboard
- Full GTM strategy and North Star metric
